# BMP180 Barometric Pressure Sensor

Bosch digital barometric pressure + temperature sensor (also usable as altimeter). Pressure 300–1100 hPa (±1 hPa), temperature −40 to 85 °C. I2C interface, fixed address 0x77. On-board regulator works with 3.3 V and 5 V logic. Common in weather stations / altitude measurement.
